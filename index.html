For best results: Start by taking your image and creating one with 100% solid black background. Then ask it to do...

Start by taking your image and creating one with 100% solid black background. Then ask it to do the same but for a color that you think contrasts the rest of the image. Orange with a blue background etc. When generating the second image, make sure you tell ai to not show any ambient lighting or reflections from the color background on the objects.

Another useful tip is to tell the ai to make the color shifts between the background and foreground glass more smooth. Also this tool runs completely in your browser, so feel free to Ctrl+S save the webpage to your computer for offline access.

This tool accepts AI images as input, but internally it does not use AI image generation. Just math for the transparency and computer vision for the watermark removal.
